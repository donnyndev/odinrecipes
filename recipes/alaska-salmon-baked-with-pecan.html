Alaska Salmon Baked with Pecan

Savor the exquisite flavors of Alaska Salmon with Pecansâ€”an extraordinary dish where succulent salmon fillets are generously coated with a crunchy pecan crust. Baked to perfection, the result is a harmonious blend of buttery salmon and the nutty goodness of pecans.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons Diljon mustard
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 5 teaspoons honey
  • .5 cups fresh bread crumbs
  • .5 cups finely chopped pecans
  • 3 teaspoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 6 (4 ounce) fillets salmon
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 6 lemon wedges

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit (200 degrees Celcius). In a small bowl, mix together the mustard, butter, and honey. In another bowl, mix together the bread crumbs, pecans, and parsley.
  2. Season each salmon fillet with salt and pepper. place on a lightly greased baking sheet. Brush with mustard-honey mixture. Cover the top of each fillet with bread crumb mixture.
  3. Bake for 10 minutes per inch of thickness, measured at the thickest part, or until salmon just flakes when tested with a fork. Serve garnished with lemon wedges.
