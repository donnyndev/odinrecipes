Grilled Cheese

Embrace the comfort of a classic Grilled Cheeseâ€”a timeless delight where layers of gooey melted cheese are sandwiched between golden, buttery slices of perfectly grilled bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 slices of Cheddar cheese
  • 1.5 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread. Place bread butter-side down on hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  2. Cook until lightly browned on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  3. Cook until lightlyu browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
